Reel Capacity Calculator

Uses the Quabbin reel factor method:
Reel factor = (H + B) × H × T × 0.262
Length (ft) = Reel factor ÷ (cable diameter in inches)²
where A = flange diameter, B = drum diameter (from circumference), T = traverse, and
H = (A − B) / 2 − 1" (assumes 1" unused flange each side).

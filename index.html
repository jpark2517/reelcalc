Reel Capacity Calculator

Uses the Quabbin Wire & Cable reel factor method:
Reel factor = (H + B) × H × T × 0.262
Length (ft) = Reel factor ÷ (cable diameter in inches)²
where:

  • A = flange diameter (in)
  • B = drum diameter (in) (calculated from circumference)
  • T = traverse (in)
  • H = build height = (A − B) / 2 − U
  • U = unused flange (fixed at 1")
You enter drum circumference; the calculator converts it to diameter internally.
